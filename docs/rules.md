# SkyJo Game Rules

## Overview

SkyJo is a card game for 2-8 players. The goal is to have the **lowest score** after all rounds.

## Setup

- **Deck**: 150 cards with values from -2 to 12
  - -2: ×5 cards
  - -1: ×10 cards
  - 0: ×15 cards
  - 1-12: ×10 cards each

- **Starting Layout**: Each player gets 12 cards arranged in a 3×4 grid, face down
- Each player reveals 2 cards of their choice
- Top card of remaining deck is placed face-up as discard pile

## Gameplay

### Turn Structure

On your turn, choose ONE:

1. **Draw from deck**:
   - Draw top card from deck
   - Then either:
     - **a)** Replace one of your 12 cards with it (old card → discard pile)
     - **b)** Discard drawn card and reveal one of your face-down cards

2. **Take from discard pile**:
   - Take top card from discard pile
   - Must replace one of your 12 cards with it (old card → discard pile)

### Column Completion

If you complete a column (3 cards) with the **same value**, that column is immediately removed and discarded (scores 0 points).

### Round End

A round ends when **one player reveals their last face-down card**. All other players get one final turn, then all remaining cards are revealed.

**Special rule**: If the player who ended the round does NOT have the lowest score, their score is doubled.

### Scoring

Each card counts its face value. Lower is better!

- Column of three identical cards: 0 points (already removed)
- All other cards: sum their values

### Game End

The game ends when **any player reaches 100+ points total** across all rounds.

The player with the **lowest total score** wins!
